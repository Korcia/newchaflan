CHAFLÁN TRAVELLER

Designed for groups of friends and couples that want to share a very "chic" weekend in Madrid.

2 nights accommodation (Friday and Saturday with departure on Sunday), in a Superior double room. First night dinner in "The Chaflan" creative tapas made by Juan Pablo Felipe, drinks included. Afterwards we continue at the the "Glass Bar" of Hotel Urban, where we can enjoy 2 drinks at one of the renown Cocktail Bar in Madrid. Now to bed and buffet breakfast in the morning.

Second night, Juan Pablo Felipe prepares a tasting menu of his most representative dishes. End the evening with 2 drinks at the Lounge.
